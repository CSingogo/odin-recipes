Classic Lasagna Recipe

Indulge in the timeless comfort of a classic lasagna, a dish that brings together layers of rich flavors and hearty goodness. This beloved recipe features perfectly cooked lasagna noodles nestled between layers of savory ground beef or Italian sausage, velvety ricotta cheese, and a robust tomato sauce. The ensemble is generously adorned with gooey melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, creating a symphony of textures and tastes in every bite. Baked to golden perfection, this lasagna is a crowd-pleaser that transcends generations, making it an ideal choice for family gatherings or cozy dinners.

Whether you're a seasoned chef or a home cook eager to embark on a culinary adventure, this classic lasagna recipe promises a satisfying and delicious experience that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound (about 450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups (500g) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups (500g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup (100g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 can (28 ounces/800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces/170g) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) red wine (optional)
  • 1/4 cup (15g) fresh basil, chopped
  • 1/4 cup (15g) fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook Noodles: Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Brown Meat: In a large skillet, brown the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat. Add chopped onions and minced garlic, and cook until the meat is browned and the onions are translucent.
  4. Prepare Sauce: Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, red wine (if using), basil, parsley, oregano, salt, and pepper to the meat mixture. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Prepare Cheese Mixture: In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, beaten eggs, and half of the shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  6. Layering: In a baking dish, start assembling the lasagna. Begin with a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, and then a layer of the cheese mixture. Repeat until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  7. Cheese Topping: Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses on top.
  8. Bake: Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes. Then, uncover and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Let it Rest: Allow the lasagna to rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing. This helps the layers set and makes it easier to serve.
  10. Serve: Slice the lasagna into portions and serve with a sprinkle of fresh parsley on top. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
